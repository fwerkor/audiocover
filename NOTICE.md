# Notice

AudioCover is an orchestration and local training framework. It does not include copyrighted songs, private recordings, or third-party model source code.

Some backend runtime packs may include pretrained third-party weights needed for local preprocessing or initialization. Third-party engines and models such as RVC, Seed-VC, So-VITS-SVC, ContentVec, Demucs, UVR, FFmpeg, and PyTorch have their own licenses and requirements. Verify them before redistribution.

For public releases of generated audio, verify that you have rights to the source song, target profile, and any generated derivative work.
